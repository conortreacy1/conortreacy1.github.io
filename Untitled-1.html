Welcome to the Manchester united supporters club

A football clubs fans are the lifeblood of the sport and support the club in more than just one way - economically, culturally, and socially. More than any other type of stakeholder, they make lifetime commitments and invest in sport. These fans invest their lblood, sweat and tears into the club. The fans are the most crucial component of the team since they are the ones that pay the players, staff, and coaches. They instill confidence in the players, and whichever their favorite team is, they adore them.

JADON SANCHO

Jadon Sancho is a professional footballer from England who currently plays as a winger for Premier League club Manchester United and the England national team. In one-on-one situations, he is recognized for his cunning, game intelligence, pace, and use of feints.

ANTHONY ELANGA

Anthony Elanga is a Swedish professional footballer who plays as a winger for Manchester United and the Sweden national team. Elanga joined Manchester United's development system at the age of 12 and was named Jimmy Murphy Young Player of the Year in 2020.

DAVID DEGEA 

David de Gea is a Spanish professional football goalkeeper who plays for Premier League club Manchester United as well as the Spain national team. Throughout the latter half of the 2010s, he was regarded as one of the finest goalkeepers in the world.
