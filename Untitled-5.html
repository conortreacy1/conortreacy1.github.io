Who are we?

This is an unoffical Manchester United Supporters Club that has been set up in March of 2022. This is a group where supporters of the club can look into the history of the club and learn about the the many events that United have endured in the past. This Manchester United Supporters Club provides a service for supporters in the northwest of Ireland including areas such as Sligo, Donegal and Leitrim. This is an excellent opportunity for United fans to meet like-minded individuals who share the same hopes and aspirations for the club. 

This page will look at the latest news while also looking at the past expeririences. This group will also look at the the best and most inform players who are playing for the club today. Even though Manchester United are in their worst period of form in a long time, we still support this team no matter what state the club is in. Manchester is red and it always will be. The impact the club has had on Greater Manchester proves this.

 Below, are some of the members who have helped with the o#setting-up and organising of the group. These individuals are avid Manchester United fans who have a real passion for the club. As a group, we have attended many United games including home and away games. The history of the club makes us proud to be supporters of the club.
